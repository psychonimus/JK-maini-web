JK Maini Precision Technology

Precision that Drives Mobility. Leadership that Shapes Industry

JK Maini Precision Technology Ltd. stands as a dominant force in the global auto components industry, delivering critical components that power the world’s most trusted mobility brands. We hold a market-leading share of over 50% in India’s passenger vehicle segment and over 45% in commercial vehicles, supplying to top OEMs and Tier-1 partners. Our leadership in ring gears, flexplates, and water pump bearings is built on decades of engineering expertise, deep partnerships, and an unrelenting focus on precision, reliability, and performance at scale. As India’s only domestic manufacturer of flexplates, fulfilling over 25% of the nation’s demand for passenger vehicles, we combine scale with specialization, positioning JK Maini as a complete partner for mission-critical powertrain and motion technologies. With advanced manufacturing, future-ready technologies, and global quality systems, we are not just meeting the needs of today’s mobility platforms, we’re engineering what’s next.
