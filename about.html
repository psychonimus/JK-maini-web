Raymond Group

A Legacy Woven with Excellence

One of India’s most respected names in branded fabric and fashion retail. Incorporated in 1925, Raymond has built a legacy of excellence in textile manufacturing, with an impressive capacity to produce 31 million meters of wool and wool-blended fabrics.

The Group owns a distinguished portfolio of apparel brands including Raymond, Premium Apparel, Raymond Made to Measure, Ethnix, Park Avenue Woman, ColorPlus, Kamasutra, and Parx. These brands are available through our exclusive retail chain, The Raymond Shop (TRS), which has a strong presence with over 700 stores across more than 200 cities in India and overseas.

About JK Maini Group

A Legacy Woven with Precision

JK Maini is a joint venture forged by two stalwarts of Indian industry — the Raymond Group and the Maini Group — combining rich legacies in precision engineering, textile excellence, and innovative manufacturing.

Founded in 1925, the Raymond Group is one of India’s most respected names in fabric and fashion, with a production capacity of over 31 million meters of wool and wool-blended suiting fabrics. With iconic brands like Raymond, Park Avenue, and ColorPlus, and a retail footprint in over 200 cities worldwide, Raymond brings with it a legacy of trust, craftsmanship, and nationwide impact.

Established in 1973, the Maini Group is a diversified conglomerate specializing in engineering-led innovation across sectors such as:

  • Precision parts manufacturing
  • Electric mobility solutions
  • Interoperable energy infrastructure via battery swapping
