Sustainable Practices

Sustainability is not an afterthought—it’s engineered into every process, material, and decision we make. From energy-efficient operations to responsible sourcing, we are committed to building a greener, smarter future through conscious manufacturing.

Sustainability Goals

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We are dedicated to building a future where business growth goes hand in hand with environmental stewardship and social responsibility. Our sustainability goals focus on reducing our carbon footprint, conserving water and energy, minimizing waste, and fostering a safe and inclusive workplace.

By embracing innovative technologies and responsible sourcing, we strive to create lasting value for our communities and the planet. Our journey is guided by transparency, accountability, and a passion for making a positive impact—today and for generations to come.

CO2 Emission Trend for Nashik BU

Driving Decarbonization: Our CO₂ Emission Reduction Journey

Over the years, our Nashik BU has consistently demonstrated its commitment to sustainable manufacturing. The CO₂ Emission Trend chart paints a compelling picture of how the company is balancing growth with environmental responsibility.

In FY20, we began its sustainability journey with a CO₂ emission index of 1.11 MT of CO₂ per MT of raw material (RM) consumption, and 12,087 MT of emissions from RM consumption. At the time, green initiatives were still nascent.

Fast forward to FY24, while RM consumption increased to 16,671 MT, the CO₂ emission index fell significantly to 0.90 a testament to our efficient operations and emission management. During this period, the company began seeing visible benefits from two key levers:

  • Energy Intensity Reduction, contributing 3,554 MT in CO₂ savings.
  • Green Energy adoption, reducing emissions by 1,058 MT.

But the most transformational shift is yet to come.

Looking ahead, our estimates for FY25 and FY26 show a sharp drop in emission intensity—0.61 and 0.53, respectively. This decline is powered by a massive scale-up of green energy initiatives, including the launch of Open Access Solar Projects (SGD-I and SBD). These are expected to drive CO₂ savings of:

  • 4,867 MT (FY25) through energy efficiency
  • 8,913 MT (FY26) through renewable energy

What’s remarkable is that even with RM consumption decreasing slightly (from 16,671 MT in FY24 to 13,401 MT in FY26 ), the emission reductions are accelerating, proving that sustainability and operational excellence can go hand in hand.

Our emission trend is not just a graph—it’s a story of deliberate choices, strategic investments, and a clear vision for a greener tomorrow. Through continuous improvements and solar adoption, We are on track to cut its emission intensity by more than 50% in just six years—making it a true leader in sustainable manufacturing within the JK Maini Group.
