JK Maini Aerospace

Our Aerospace Capabilities

With over two decades of aerospace expertise, JK Maini is a trusted name in precision-engineered components for global aerospace leaders. Backed by the legacy of Raymond Group and the innovation-led heritage of Maini, we deliver high-complexity, flight-critical parts with world-class quality, speed, and scale.

From stator vanes and engine housings to flap tracks and fuel system assemblies, we engineer over 1200+ SKUs across complex engine, structural, and system components. Our 10,500 sq. m. aerospace facility in Bengaluru is built to meet the rigorous standards of global OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers.

One Stop Solution for

Aircraft Components

We manufacture a wide range of high-precision aircraft components that meet the rigorous standards of the aerospace industry. Our expertise includes the production of critical parts such as landing gear components, structural assemblies, engine mounts, and precision-machined fittings for both fixed-wing and rotary aircraft.

With advanced manufacturing capabilities and strict quality controls, we supply OEMs and Tier-1 aerospace suppliers with components engineered for safety, reliability, and performance. Our solutions support commercial, defense, and helicopter programs worldwide, helping our partners achieve new heights in aviation excellence.
