Core Components & Systems

We manufacture a wide array of precision components—from ground pins and bar-stock turned parts to full sub-assemblies. Whether you need engine parts, transmission components, hydraulic fittings, or ready-to-install assemblies, we provide end-to-end manufacturing—from heat treatment and surface coatings to JIT logistics and final delivery

Materials, Tolerances & Volume

  • Materials: Carbon & alloy steels, special tool steels, and non-ferrous metals (copper, brass, aluminum)
  • Typical Tolerances: Diameter ±0.001 mm, concentricity between OD/ID ±0.005 mm
  • Surface Finish: Ra ≤ 0.4, hardness 61–63 HRC
  • Production Scale: Single-piece prototypes to high-volume runs (up to 1 million precision-ground pins per month)

Quality & Standards Compliance

We integrate Advanced Product Quality Planning (APQP) and Failure Mode Effect Analysis (FMEA) throughout our processes. Tooling is designed in-house using CATIA and AutoCAD for maximum accuracy; assemblies are tested in dedicated fixtures under documented functional protocols
