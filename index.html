JK Maini Precision Technology Ltd.

JK Maini Precision technology limited has been at the forefront of precision engineering, enabling how the world moves and builds across industries. From high-performance automotive components to complex solutions for industrial, defense, and general engineering applications, our expertise drives progress where reliability and performance are non-negotiable. Trusted by global leaders. Chosen for precision. Built to deliver. Our solutions power critical systems across some of the world's most demanding sectors — supporting everything from traditional mobility platforms to the evolving needs of manufacturing, infrastructure, and defense. With advanced facilities, robust processes, and an unwavering commitment to quality, we deliver precision at scale, with consistency our customers rely on.

JK Maini Global Aerospace Ltd.

JK Maini Global Aerospace Ltd. (JKMGAL) specializes in delivering highprecision components, complex assemblies, and mission-critical systems for the global aerospace and defense industries. Our solutions support the world's most demanding platforms — from commercial and military aviation to defense systems operating in the most challenging environments. With advanced infrastructure, globally certified processes, and deep technical expertise, we enable performance, safety, and reliability at scale. We are trusted by OEMs, Tier 1s, and defense organizations worldwide for our unwavering focus on precision, consistency, and quality. From complex geometries to tight tolerances and advanced materials, we deliver with excellence — with precision, without compromise.
